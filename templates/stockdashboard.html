001 : Stock Dashboard


Stock dashboard for visualising stocks of the FAANG companies and predicting stock price. The dashboard was created using python, dash and scikit-learn, The goal of the project was to provide users with an interactive dashboard for displaying various graphs which can be downloaded and to give an option to compare companies as well as to predict the close price based on the open price.

In a world where investing in the stock market can be in a snap of the finger bypassing brokers, commissions and delay of information, it has become much easier to lose your fortunes in the snap of the finger.

Ever since the digital era began, access to information was widespread however similarly the information being spread had no filters. Similarly many people invest in the stock market because of a certain “tip” they received online from a self proclaimed guru claiming the stock would soar high and make them financially independent. However, in reality, the majority of these tips were professional “pump and dump” schemes used in certain penny stocks which resulted in the ordinary investors' wealth being wiped off.

Although there are several programs and websites that do offer such information, it is important to know that the average investor is not savvy and does not understand how the data functions and what it represents. Unfortunately most are not bothered either to understand as it requires time and energy to study and understand.

By using a series of different data representations, I have developed a web based solution which not only shows complex data but with the assistance of our complex algorithms, one can predict the stock price based on historical data.
